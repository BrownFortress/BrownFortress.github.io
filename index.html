Who am I ?

I'm a Ph.D student at the University of Trento.

Hello! This is Gabriel. I’m in the last year of a PhD course in computer science and information technology at the University of Trento. My research is currently focused on natural language processing, particularly language understanding, in the areas of natural language generation, affective computing, and dialogue systems.

Active research projects

I’m currently working on two research projects:

  • Affective computing: We are studying the relationship between biosignals and emotions expressed in a personal narrative to model the emotions from the point of view of the narrator. (A personal narrative is the recount of a past emotionally charged event lived by the narrator.)
  • Temporal Reasoning: After having assessed that LLMs underperform in extracting temporal relations (work presented in my latest article), we’re investigating whether LLMs can leverage additional information conveyed by images to predict the temporal relation between two events.
